    • Electric grid construction site safety monitoring
      Jun. 2021 - Sep. 2021

      • Monitor the safety belt usage for personnel working at heights by Faster RCNN model.
      • Implemented safety monitoring logic based on IOU between person and safety belt bounding boxes.
      • Improved model performance with anchor prior and CutMix data augmentation, resulting in an increased mAR_50 from 92% to 95% and a final mAP_50 of 98%.

    • Open Source Project: OpenMMLab Framwork Development
      Jun. 2022 - Jun. 2023

      • Applied post-training quantization to the Hourglass pose detection model, converting it from FP32 to INT8. This resulted in a roughly 30% increase in inference speed when using TensorRT, OpenVINO, and ncnn.
      • eparated the RTMDet ONNX model, removed its post-processing part, and implemented post-processing with the MMDeploy SDK. The model was then successfully deployed on the RV1126 embedded device.
      • Added new operators introduced in Yolov6 3.0 and Yolov6-face, conducted unit tests to ensure their functionality, and verified the accuracy of the reproduced model to be consistent with the original model.
